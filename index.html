Caroline Jacomini Conde

Architect | 3D Environment Artist | Immersive Media

I'm a 3D artist enthusiastic about creating virtual experiences where level design and environmental art play a crucial role in making gameplay immersive. As an architect, 3D mockups are essential for understanding the dimensions and feel of the constructed space. In virtual worlds, there are no constraints to creativity, as long as you keep everything running smoothly.
